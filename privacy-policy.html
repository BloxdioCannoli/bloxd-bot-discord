Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bloxd Bot collects, processes, stores, and uses information.

Information Collected

Depending on the Bot's features, Bloxd Bot may collect, process, store, and analyze any information made available through the Discord API, including but not limited to:

  • User IDs
  • Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Message IDs
  • Message content
  • Usernames, global display names, nicknames, avatars, and profile information made available by Discord
  • Command usage
  • Interaction data
  • Permissions
  • Server configuration
  • Audit-related information
  • Timestamps
  • Moderation actions
  • Logs generated through Bot usage
  • Metadata associated with Discord events
  • Any additional information provided through commands, forms, buttons, modals, or other interactions with the Bot

Purpose of Collection

Collected information may be used to:

  • Provide Bot functionality.
  • Store server settings.
  • Improve existing features.
  • Develop new features.
  • Detect abuse, spam, fraud, or malicious activity.
  • Debug errors.
  • Generate analytics and usage statistics.
  • Maintain security.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to operate, improve, secure, and maintain the Bot, unless otherwise required by law or deleted at the developer's discretion.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared:

  • When required by law.
  • To comply with legal requests.
  • To protect the security or integrity of the Bot.
  • With service providers that assist in operating the Bot, where applicable.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

User Rights

Where applicable under law, users may request information regarding data associated with their Discord account or request deletion of stored data, subject to legal obligations and legitimate operational requirements.

Children's Privacy

The Bot is intended for use through Discord. Users should comply with Discord's age requirements.
